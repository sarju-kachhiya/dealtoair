Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

This Privacy Policy explains how Dealtoair (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects the information you provide when using our website dealtoair.com (“the Site”). This policy applies solely to information gathered through this Site and not to third-party websites linked from here.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect personal information you provide when requesting products or services through the Site. For example, when seeking quotes, you may be asked for details such as your full name, address, telephone number, email, and other relevant information. This helps us connect you with service providers and agents.

We may also collect demographic data (such as gender or zip code) that is not tied to your personal identity. Additionally, we may receive supplemental information about you from other online or offline sources and link it with your existing account.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • Quote Requests: If you request quotes on the Site, the details you provide may be shared with our trusted network of carriers, agents, lead aggregators, and service providers to fulfill your request. These partners may contact you by phone, email, SMS/text, or mail—even if you are on a national or state “Do Not Call” list.
  • Aggregate Data: We may share non-personal, aggregated, or de-identified information with third parties for purposes such as targeted advertising and performance analysis.
  • Legal Requirements: Your information may be disclosed if required by law, such as in response to subpoenas, audits, investigations, or other legal proceedings.
  • Business Transfers: If Dealtoair merges with or is acquired by another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Cookies and IP Addresses

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience by reducing page load times, personalizing your journey, and tracking site usage. Cookies may also help deliver targeted advertising. We also log IP addresses for system administration, fraud detection, and tracking overall site performance.

Emails & SMS Communication

By registering on our site or through a partner, you may receive promotional emails or newsletters from us. You can opt out anytime by clicking the unsubscribe link.

We also offer an SMS service, where you may receive promotional or service-related text messages. Standard carrier charges may apply. By opting in, you confirm you are the authorized user of the device.

Data Security

Your personal information is protected with physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards in compliance with federal standards. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the CCPA, including:

  • The right to know what personal data is collected and how it is used.
  • The right to know whether your data is sold or disclosed, and to whom.
  • The right to opt-out of data sales.
  • The right to access and request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to equal service, even if you exercise your privacy rights.
  • The right to designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf.

To make a CCPA request, contact us at 312-212-0044.
